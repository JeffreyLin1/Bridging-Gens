Patricia

Canadian-raised, daughter of a Chinese Immigrant

Patricia is a current eleventh grade student in Toronto. Raised by her mainland Chinese mother, she names the generational and cultural gap as the biggest differences between the two of them. “They were raised in a kind of stoic manner. So, they’re not used to the kind of western-way of expressing yourself.” Involved in both photography and writing, Patricia’s values for artistic expression contrast her mother’s own foreignity towards said principles. What might have led Patricia’s ability to express herself outgrow her mother’s contrasting way of thinking?
