Llen

Chinese & Canadian-raised, child of immigrants

Llen is a current tenth grade student in Toronto. Llen immigrated to Canada in [year], early on in their childhood. One of their main commitments is recreational dance at the downtown studio, DanceLifeX. While Llen identifies their dance community as hugely significant, they also recognize the dissonance with friends and family back in China since they moved, including their older brother that never moved with Llen’s family. Similarly to other interviewees, Llen links the moral and ethical disagreements between them and their parents to cultural differences. “They’re already at a grown age so it’s difficult to change the way they think. So it’s easier to just agree”. What might Llen’s willingness to accept their parents’ opposing beliefs be a result of?
